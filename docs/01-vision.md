# 01 — Vision

## The opportunity

Texas's three biggest metros — Houston, Dallas–Fort Worth, San Antonio — contain hundreds of thousands of small service businesses: HVAC contractors, landscapers, detailers, barbershops, taquerias, roofers, plumbers, cleaning companies. A large slice of them either:

1. **Have no website at all** — a Facebook page and a phone number, or just a Google Business Profile, or
2. **Have a dead site** — built in 2012, not mobile-friendly, wrong hours, broken contact form, no SSL.

These businesses lose customers every day to competitors who look legitimate online. They know it, but they believe a website costs $5k+ and months of hassle, or they got burned once by a freelancer who disappeared.

## What we do

We find them systematically (scraping public listings + automated website audits), reach out with a **personalized audit of their current online presence**, and sell a fast, fixed-price, modern website with ongoing hosting and maintenance as recurring revenue.

Our unfair advantage is **AI-powered delivery**: with Claude Code building sites, one person can deliver in hours what a traditional agency quotes weeks for. Our cost of goods is close to zero, so we can undercut agencies on price while beating freelancers on speed and professionalism.

## The wedge

- **Speed:** "Your new site live in 7 days" (internally we can do it in 1–2).
- **Proof up front:** we show the prospect a mockup/demo of THEIR business's new homepage before they pay anything. Seeing their own name on a beautiful site closes deals.
- **Recurring floor:** every deal includes a monthly care plan (hosting, edits, updates). One-time builds pay the bills; the monthly base is the business.

## The goal

- **Phase 1 (proof):** 5 paying clients. Validate pricing, delivery time, and the outreach funnel.
- **Phase 2 (system):** dashboard-driven pipeline, repeatable weekly cadence: N leads scraped → M contacted → X demos → Y closes.
- **Phase 3 (scale):** enough monthly recurring revenue that the base covers real salaries; expand niches/cities, consider hiring or productizing.

Concrete numbers for each phase live in `config/targets.json` and `docs/08-roadmap.md`.

## What we are NOT

- Not a "full-service digital marketing agency" on day one. We sell **websites + care plans**. Upsells (Google Business optimization, SEO basics, review management) come later, listed in the business model doc as expansion revenue.
- Not custom software. Templatized, beautiful, fast small-business sites. Scope creep is the killer; the delivery playbook defines exactly what's included.
